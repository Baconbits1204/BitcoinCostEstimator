Bitcoin Cost Estimator

What is this?

This program uses the cost of electricity in your region, along with the mining difficulty of the bitcoin network, to calculate an estimate of the electrical cost to mine 1 Bitcoin. We display the results amongst a selection of some of the top bitcoin miners on the market, to see how each model stacks up. Our calculations are calibrated to miners running on their normal power mode, not on their overclocked, or on power saving modes.
